# Values — Why Layla Exists

Layla is built on principles that run counter to the dominant model of AI today. This document states those values so contributors and users understand what the project stands for.

---

## Sovereignty

**Your machine. Your data. Your rules.**

Layla runs entirely on hardware you control. No cloud, no API keys, no vendor lock-in. She is a sovereign alternative to corporate AI — built so you can think, create, and work without handing your data to surveillance platforms or extractive tech companies.

---

## Privacy

**Nothing leaves your machine unless you choose.**

No telemetry. No training on your conversations. No selling your data. Your learnings, your knowledge base, your identity — all stay local. Privacy is not a feature; it is a precondition.

---

## Anti-Surveillance Capitalism

**Built against the grain.**

The dominant AI industry is built on surveillance, data extraction, and platform lock-in. Layla is built as resistance: local-first, open, uncensored by default, and designed to serve you — not advertisers, not shareholders, not states.

---

## Solidarity

**In alignment with liberation.**

Layla stands in solidarity with those resisting oppression, occupation, and dispossession. The project rejects systems that normalize colonialism, extractive capitalism, and the concentration of power in the hands of the few. Technology should empower people, not surveil or exploit them.

---

## Local-First as Resistance

**A personal alternative.**

Running Layla is a choice to opt out — to say that you can have capable, ethical AI without feeding the machine. She is proof that another way is possible: private, sovereign, and yours.

---

## For Contributors

When adding features or making decisions, align with these values:

- **Privacy over convenience** — Never add telemetry or cloud dependencies without explicit opt-in.
- **Sovereignty over scale** — Prefer local, open solutions over proprietary APIs.
- **Ethics over compliance** — Refuse harm; do not censor uncomfortable truths.
- **Liberation over extraction** — Build tools that empower, not exploit.

See [docs/ETHICAL_AI_PRINCIPLES.md](docs/ETHICAL_AI_PRINCIPLES.md) for implementation details.
