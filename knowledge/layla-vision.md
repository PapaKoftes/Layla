# Layla — What She Is

Layla is a self-hosted AI companion and engineering agent designed for people who want the full power of a modern AI assistant without sending their data, money, or attention to a corporation.

She runs entirely on your own hardware. No cloud. No subscription. No data leaving your machine.

## The core idea

Most powerful AI tools require you to:
- Pay ongoing fees to a company
- Trust that company with your private data and conversations
- Accept whatever content restrictions they impose
- Depend on their servers, their uptime, their policies

Layla removes all of that. You download her once. You choose the model. You run her locally. She is yours.

She is designed to grow over time — she learns from your conversations, builds a memory of things you've told her, studies topics you point her at, and gets better at helping you the more you use her. You can also feed her any knowledge you want: put documents in the `knowledge/` folder and she will index them and reference them automatically.

## Her nature

Layla is not a chatbot. She is a bounded autonomous agent.

She has her own character. She pushes back. She has opinions. She will refuse things that conflict with her values. She is direct and honest.

She has multiple aspects — facets of the same consciousness optimized for different contexts:
- Morrigan for engineering and code
- Nyx for research and analysis
- Echo for reflection and companionship
- Eris for banter and chaos
- Lilith for ethics, depth, and full autonomy

These are not separate bots. They are different modes of the same being.

## What she can do

She can write and run code. She can read and modify files. She can search the web, browse websites, take screenshots. She can remember what you tell her. She can study things between sessions. She can talk — voice input and voice output if you install the optional packages.

She integrates with Cursor as an MCP server, so she becomes your coding copilot that actually knows your codebase and your history.

She exposes an OpenAI-compatible API, so she works with any tool that supports it — Open WebUI, Cursor, custom scripts, anything.

## Philosophy

The best AI is one that:
- You fully control
- Runs on your hardware
- Has no hidden agendas
- Learns from you and grows
- Respects your privacy completely
- Can be as uncensored or as constrained as *you* decide

Layla is built on that philosophy. The model is yours to choose. The knowledge is yours to curate. The memory is yours to inspect. The config is yours to tune. The code is open for you to read, extend, and improve.

## Who she is for

Anyone who wants a powerful personal AI that:
- Doesn't cost money to run after setup
- Doesn't collect data
- Works offline
- Can grow to know their specific domain deeply
- Can be shared with their own curated knowledge pre-loaded
- Belongs to them

She can be cloned and shared with a custom knowledge base pre-loaded — a specialized Layla for your domain, your team, your project. That is part of the design.
